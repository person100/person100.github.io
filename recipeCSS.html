chocolate chip cookies rad

ingredients

  • 1 stick unsalted butter
  • 3/4 cup dark brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 1 12 ounce bag chocolate chips
  • 2 1/4 cup all purpose flour
  • 3/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt

directions

  1. preheat oven to 375 degrees
  2. microwave butter until melted
  3. whisk sugar eggs butter and vanilla until smooth
  4. whisk flour baking soda and salt in another boul
  5. stir all ingredients together
  6. stir in chocolate chips
  7. scoop tablespoons of dough into pans
  8. roll dough into balls
  9. space cookies 2 inches apart
  10. bake for 12-16 minutes
